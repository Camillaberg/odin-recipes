Ramen

Heart warming and spicy ramen for the cold autmn weather.

Text about this dish and how it helped me through life

Ingridients 2 people

  • 2 Eggs
  • 1Pk Ramen Noodles
  • Sirracha Sauce
  • Two handfull of Spinach
  • 1/2 Romaine Lettuce
  • Chicken broth
  • Sesame Seeds

Steps:

  1. Boil two pots of water, one with 300 mL and one full pot.
  2. In the full pot add the eggs and let boil for 7 minutes. In the other add noodles and broth, let boil for 4 minutes.
  3. After two minutes of boiling noodles, add spinach.
  4. Cut up some lettuce or other vegetable of interest.
  5. Once done, peel egg and decorate with egg and vegatble. Add Siracha and Sesame seeds to taste
