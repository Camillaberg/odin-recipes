Ingridients 2 people

  • 1pk feta cheese
  • 1pk cherry tomatoes
  • Olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • Pasta of chouce

Steps:

  1. In a oven save dish, add the tomatoes and garlic with some olive oil and mix.
  2. Add a pinch of salt and a pinch of pepper.
  3. In the middle of the dish place the whole feta cheese and put dish in the middle of the oven on 200Â°C for 20 minutes.
  4. While dish is in the oven boil pasta following the package instructions. keep some pasta water.
  5. Using a fork, mix and squish the tomatoes, feta and garlic. Mix in some pasta water before adding the pasta.

Optional: add spinach to the feta mixture before adding pasta
